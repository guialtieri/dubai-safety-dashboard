📎 Data Sources & Methodology

Methodology: The Safety Score is calculated using a weighted algorithm: Kinetic/Military (35%), Infrastructure/Logistics (25%), Diplomatic Advisories (20%), Economic/Flight pricing (10%), Social/Closures (10%). Each sub-score ranges 0–100, adjusted per neighborhood based on local debris history and target proximity. Data refreshed daily at 08:00 GST. Flight prices are indicative. This dashboard is for informational purposes only and does not replace official government advisories.

Full Color Guide

Safety Score Zones

Safe Zone (75–100)

Normal vigilance. Continue daily routines with standard awareness.

Caution Zone (50–74)

Elevated risk indicators. Stay informed and ensure essential documents are accessible. Consider your personal risk tolerance.

Warning Zone (25–49)

Multiple risk factors active. Consider relocating to a safer area within the UAE. Prepare go-bags.

Critical Zone (0–24)

Immediate departure recommended. Book the next available flight. Contact your embassy.

Orange
